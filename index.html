GK VIMFlow

AI-Powered Invoice Autofill for the IOCL Vendor Invoice Management (VIM) Portal

GK VIMFlow is a Chrome Extension that helps vendors automate invoice submission on the IOCL Vendor Invoice Management (VIM) portal. Simply upload an invoice PDF, review the extracted information, and automatically populate invoice fields with a single click.

Features

  • AI Invoice Extraction
  • OCR for Scanned PDFs
  • Automatic Form Filling
  • Invoice Data Review
  • Fast & Secure
  • Easy to Use
