Frequently Asked Questions

Is GK VIMFlow free?

Basic features are available for free. Premium features may be offered in future versions.

Does it upload my invoices?

Only user-selected invoice files are processed. Data handling depends on the enabled features and user actions.

Is this an official IOCL product?

No. GK VIMFlow is an independent productivity extension and is not affiliated with Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL).
